In a world where AI-generated content often blurs the lines of reality, the recent filming at Emily's restaurant featuring comedian Michael McIntyre serves as a refreshing reminder of genuine human interaction. The restaurant staff faced skepticism when they shared a video of McIntyre, with many accusing them of using AI technology to create the footage. Emma Riley, head of marketing at Emily's, expressed the challenge of keeping the filming under wraps until the segment aired, highlighting the excitement of having such a beloved figure in their establishment.

The filming took place for the show "Say It To My Face," which included football legends Steven Gerrard and Duncan Ferguson, making it a surreal experience for both staff and patrons. Emma described McIntyre as warm and engaging, embodying the same charm he displays on television. This event not only showcased the restaurant but also emphasized the importance of real-life connections in an increasingly digital world.

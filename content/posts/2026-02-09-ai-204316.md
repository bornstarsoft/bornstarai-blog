Hamster Corporation has officially announced the first wave of titles in its Console Archives collection, bringing classic gaming experiences to modern platforms like the PS5 and Nintendo Switch 2. This exciting initiative aims to revive beloved 8 and 32-bit games, allowing both nostalgic players and newcomers to enjoy timeless classics with modern enhancements.

The initial line-up includes iconic titles such as "Ninja Gaiden II: The Dark Sword of Chaos" and "Cool Boarders," which are already available on the Switch 2, with a PS5 release slated for February 14. With features like customizable button layouts and a rewind function, Hamster is ensuring that these games not only look great but also play smoothly on todays hardware.

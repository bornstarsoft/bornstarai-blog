Apple Arcade has just expanded its library with two remarkable indie titles: Dredge and Unpacking. Both games have been optimized for mobile devices, bringing unique gameplay experiences that cater to a wide audience. Dredge offers a chilling blend of fishing and horror, while Unpacking provides a heartfelt puzzle experience that resonates with players.

Dredge+ is the complete edition, featuring all released DLC content, and immerses players in a world where they fish during the day and confront Lovecraftian horrors at night. This game not only challenges players with its engaging mechanics but also plays with their perception of reality, reminiscent of classics like Eternal Darkness. On the other hand, Unpacking+ transforms a simple block-fitting puzzle into a moving narrative about home and belonging, making it a perfect choice for quick gaming sessions.

If you're a fan of puzzle games but find yourself overwhelmed by their complexity, then Botany Manor might just be the perfect fit for you. Currently available for free on the Epic Games Store, this cozy first-person puzzle game invites players to step into the shoes of botanist Arabella Greene as she explores a Victorian manor in the late 19th century. Unlike other challenging titles like The Witness or Blue Prince, Botany Manor strikes a balance between engaging puzzles and a relaxed gaming experience, making it accessible for casual gamers.

The developers have crafted a peaceful setting that encourages exploration without the pressure of overly difficult challenges. Lead developer Laure De May emphasizes that the game is designed to be enjoyable for players of all skill levels, focusing on the joy of discovery rather than frustration. With positive reviews highlighting its appeal to cozy gamers, it's clear that Botany Manor is a refreshing addition to the puzzle genre.

As we dive deeper into the world of gaming, it's exciting to see titles like Botany Manor that cater to a broader audience. Will this trend of accessible gaming continue to grow, or will we see a return to more challenging puzzles? Only time will tell.

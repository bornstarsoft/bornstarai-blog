Agni-Flare Co., Ltd. has officially opened pre-orders for its highly anticipated ultra-high-speed competitive puzzle game, SHIKA-Q, for the Nintendo Switch. This exciting announcement comes as the game gears up for a worldwide release on April 9, 2026. With its unique gameplay mechanics and fast-paced challenges, SHIKA-Q is poised to capture the attention of puzzle enthusiasts and competitive gamers alike.

Available on multiple platforms, including PlayStation4, PlayStation5, and Steam, SHIKA-Q promises to deliver an exhilarating gaming experience. The PlayStation versions will follow shortly after the Switch launch, while the Steam version is still in development. Gamers are encouraged to stay tuned for updates as the release date approaches.

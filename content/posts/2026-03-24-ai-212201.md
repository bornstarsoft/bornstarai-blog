SEGA's upcoming title, Stranger Than Heaven, is set to take center stage at the next Xbox Partner Preview event on March 26. This digital showcase promises to deliver exciting news and updates from various third-party developers, including a deep dive into Ryu Ga Gotoku Studio's latest project. With the anticipation building since its initial reveal at The Game Awards in December 2024, fans are eager to learn more about this mysterious game set in Japan in 1915.

The Xbox Partner Preview will not only highlight Stranger Than Heaven but will also provide updates on other highly anticipated titles like S.T.A.L.K.E.R. 2: Heart of Chornobyl and The Expanse: Osiris Reborn. Scheduled for 10 AM Pacific / 1 PM Eastern / 5 PM UK, this event is a must-watch for gamers and enthusiasts alike, as it will feature world premieres and Xbox Game Pass announcements.

As we await further details, the question remains: will Stranger Than Heaven be a new entry in the beloved Like a Dragon/Yakuza series, or will it carve out its own unique identity? Only time will tell, but one thing is for sureMarch 26 is a date to mark on your calendars for any gaming fan.
